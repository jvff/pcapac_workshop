Amdahl's Law
============

- Overall speedup by increasing parallelism
    - \\( S(s) = \frac{1}{(1-p) + \frac{1}{s}} \\)
    - \\(S\\) overall speedup
    - \\(p\\) percent that is parallel
    - \\(s\\) available parallel speedup
- Speedup limit
    - \\( \lim_{s \to \infty} S(s) = \frac{1}{1-p} \\)
